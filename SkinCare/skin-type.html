Normal Skin

If your skin is neither too dry nor too oily and it has regular texture, no imperfections and a clean,soft appearance, and does not need special care. Then you have Normal Skin.

Sensitive Skin

If your skin is more prone to react to stimuli, and it needs more care to fight dryness, roughness and its usual appearance. Then you have Sensitive Skin.

Dry Skin

If your skin doesn't retain sufficient moisture. Then you have Dry Skin.

Oily Skin

If your skin has a porous, humid and bright appearance. Then you have Oily Skin.
